About Us

Bridge to Justice believes that, Equality and Justice are the Integral Parts of Right to Life of Every Human in the Universe. We are the Bridge between the People, whose Rights are Infringed and the Court of Justice. We Support the People to Fight their Legal Battle before Judicial, Quasi - Judicial and Administrative Forums.

If you have any Legal Problem, we have the Solution.

Who We Are ?

We are a Group of Experience Advocates, who Professionally render Service and Support to the People in the Legal Battle of Justice before the Hon'ble Supreme Court of India, High Courts in India, High Court at Calcutta and District Courts, Tribunals, Arbitrators, Quasi - Judicial and Administrative Forums in India.

Our Professionals are Vastly Experience in Handling Diverse Range of Legal Matters before the Court of Justice. We have Expertise in Litigations, Conveyance & Agreement and Liaisoning.

Our Mission

We always Render our Services to the People whose Lawful Rights are Infringed and who are in any Legal Problem. Bridge to Justice always Raised Hands of Free Legal Services to the Distress People to Secure their Lawful Rights. We believe JUSTICE IS GOD..

Our Vision

We strongly believe Right to Justice, Equality before the Law and Equal Protection by the Law are Fundamental Rights of Everyone Irrespective of any Gender, Religion, Caste etc. In the Democracy Everyone should have Prompt Access to Justice to Secure their Legal Rights.
